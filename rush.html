    Yale Russian Chorus Rush 2018

    Welcome Potential Chorister!

    We're so excited that you're interested in joining North America's oldest Slavic choir!

    Since our founding in the midst of the Cold War, we've been bridging the cultural gap between America and Russia on a musical level. Even with rocky Russo-American relations today, our music inspires appreciation for Russian music and culture for generations to come.

    We in the Yale Russian Chorus hope you'll join us for our 65th year of celebrating Russian music and culture in America.

    Audition Process

    Auditioning for the YRC is simple and straightforward! First you'll do some warm-up exercises with the rest of the group so your voice is all set for some sweet notes. Then weâ€™ll see how you do on a couple of really basic musicality exercises.

    Coming after exercises is singing a short bit of a song from our repertoire. We'll choose an easy, catchy song at random for you to sing with you!

    The culmination of your audition will be your solo. Choose a thirty-second to one-minute section of any song that you're comfortable singing. This may be the most nerve-inducing part of the process, but seriously, don't stress! We just want to hear you show off your beautiful voice!
