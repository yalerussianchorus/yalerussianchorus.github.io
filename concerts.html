    Ukraine's Revolution of Dignity, 5-Year Anniversary and Holomodor Remembrance Event University of New Haven Bucknall Theatre

    As we wind down for the semester, join the Yale Russian Chorus, pianist Victor Markiew, and the Statera Quartet for a solemn memorial of Holomodor, the man-made famine that killed millions in Soviet Ukraine from 1932-3.     SUN 10/14
    2:00PM

    65th Anniversary Concert Woolsey Hall, Yale University

    Can you believe we're already celebrating the big 6-5? The Yale Russian Chorus, in conjunction with the Alumni of the Yale Russian Chorus, will mark this momentous occasion with a concert on October 14 in Yale's Woolsey Hall. Over 100 singers are expected, led by our founding conductor - Denis Mickiewicz, who began his work with the chorus in 1953. Slavic music on this scale is heard very rarely around the world, and in the United States - almost never. It will be an unforgettable event.

    This event is supported by the Yale Institute of Sacred Music
