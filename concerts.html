YRC Concerts 2022-2023

SEP 11
SUN, 3PM EST
Yale Russian Chorus 69th Anniversary Alumni Concert
Battell Chapel
Friends, the Yale Russian Chorus and Alumni are excited to invite to our 69th anniversary concert! This will be the first time since 2019 that Yale Russian Chorus and its alumni will perform together in concert. We have prepared a rousing set of love songs, folk music, and liturgical pieces. Join us in song and merriment!

All donations will go towards Doctors Without Borders to aid their noble efforts in Ukraine.
