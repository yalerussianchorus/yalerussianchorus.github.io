You've reached the home of the Yale Russian Chorus, North America's oldest Slavic choir.

From Azerbaijan to the White House, from the Russian Steppes to Carnegie Hall -- and now on the internet! -- the Yale Russian Chorus has delighted audiences across the world for 63 years.
