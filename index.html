You've reached the home of the Yale Russian Chorus, North America's oldest Slavic choir.

From Azerbaijan to the White House, from the Russian Steppes to Carnegie Hall -- and now on the internet! -- the Yale Russian Chorus has delighted audiences across the world for 64 years.

Congratulations to Beau, Claire, Dan, Ladi, Max, Manoj, Michael Michelle, Raymond, Tom, and Zach, our new members from Fall 2017 auditions!
